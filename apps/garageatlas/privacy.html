Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Garage Atlas handles information when you use the app.

Summary:

  • Garage Atlas does not require you to create an account.
  • Your vehicle and service record data is stored locally on your device.
  • The free version of the app displays non-personalised advertisements using Google AdMob.
  • Garage Atlas does not use tracking or personalised ads.
  • Garage Atlas offers an optional one-time Pro purchase through Apple’s App Store.

1. Information We Collect

Garage Atlas may handle the following types of information:

  • Vehicle names, mileage, service history, repair history, upgrade history, costs, notes, and attachments you add inside the app.
  • Purchase information related to the optional Garage Atlas Pro upgrade, handled through Apple’s App Store.
  • Limited advertising-related data collected by Google AdMob in the free version of the app.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

Garage Atlas is designed to work primarily with data stored locally on your device. The information you enter, such as vehicle records and attachments, is kept on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

3. Advertising

The free version of Garage Atlas includes banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Garage Atlas uses non-personalised ads only. This means:

  • No personalised advertising is used.
  • No app tracking permission prompt is used.
  • Garage Atlas does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Google AdMob may collect limited device and usage-related information in order to display and operate ads in the free version of the app.

4. In-App Purchases

Garage Atlas offers an optional Garage Atlas Pro one-time purchase through Apple’s App Store. Payment processing and purchase handling are managed by Apple. Garage Atlas does not store your payment card details.

5. Data Sharing

Garage Atlas does not sell your personal data.

Data may be shared only with third-party services used to operate parts of the app, such as:

  • Apple for in-app purchases
  • Google AdMob for non-personalised advertising in the free version

6. Tracking

Garage Atlas does not use tracking for advertising purposes and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

7. Your Choices

  • You can use the free version of the app with ads.
  • You can upgrade to Garage Atlas Pro to remove ads and unlock Pro features.
  • You can delete app data from your device at any time by deleting records in the app or removing the app from your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

Garage Atlas is not directed specifically to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact
