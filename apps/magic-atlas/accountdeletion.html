Magic Atlas

Delete Your Magic Atlas Account

Magic Atlas allows users to sign in using Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In via Firebase Authentication.

If you would like to delete your account, you may request deletion by contacting support.

How to request account deletion

Email support@mind-mirage.uk
Subject Magic Atlas Account Deletion Request
Include Please include the email address used in the app so the account can be located.

What happens next

After the request is reviewed and verified, the Magic Atlas account and associated app data linked to that account will be permanently deleted.

Requests are typically processed within 7 days.
