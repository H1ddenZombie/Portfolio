Magic Atlas Privacy Policy

Magic Atlas is an app developed by MindMirage.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the Magic Atlas mobile application.

Account Sign-In

Magic Atlas allows users to sign in using:

  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign-In

Authentication is managed through Firebase Authentication.

When signing in, basic account information such as your email address and authentication identifier may be used to create and manage your account within the app.

Information Collected

The app may collect limited information necessary for the operation of the service, including:

  • Email address used for authentication
  • Account identifier provided by Firebase Authentication
  • Basic usage data required for app functionality

How Information Is Used

The information collected is used only for providing core app functionality including:

  • Allowing users to sign in securely
  • Maintaining user progress and preferences within the app
  • Improving app stability and performance

Third-Party Services

Magic Atlas uses third-party services necessary to operate the app:

  • Firebase Authentication for user account management
  • Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for authentication

If advertising is shown within the free version of the app, advertising partners such as Google AdMob may collect information required to deliver advertisements.

Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the functionality of the Magic Atlas application or until a deletion request is received.
